3Düwelsteene

The 3D reconstructions of the megalithic grave "Devilstones"


Reconstructions

In 1932 the Düwelsteene were restored after an archaeological excavation. On this website time is now turned back digitally. It is possible to see the Düwelsteene as a 3D scan, as the megalithic tomb can be found today. The grave can also be seen as a 3D reconstruction, in the state that it probably was in before the restoration in 1932. Another virtual reconstruction shows the megalithic tomb of the Funnel Beaker Culture as it might have looked around 5000 years ago.

About

Directions to the megalithic tomb, information about the restoration of the tomb in 1932 and the current 3D reconstructions, information about the excavation history, as well as tales about the origin of the megalithic tomb and the origin of the name Düwelsteene.
